Our Story

About Sōjiga

Sōjiga (ಸೋಜಿಗ) — Kannada for “wonder” or “amazement”

Born from a deep love for baking and a desire to create moments of wonder, Sōjiga Bakehouse is the dream of Chef Anu — a passionate baker who believes that every loaf, every cake, and every pastry should tell a story.

What started as weekend experiments in a home kitchen has blossomed into an artisan bakehouse that marries traditional Indian flavors with global baking techniques. Each creation at Sōjiga is crafted with premium ingredients, time-honored methods, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

“I want people to taste my bakes and feel that sense of sōjiga — that moment of pure wonder when something simple becomes extraordinary.” — Chef Anu, Founder
